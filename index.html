How significant is food production's contribution to our total global greenhouse gas emissions? Accounting for 26% of global emissions, it is indeed significant. In general, animal-based foods have a greater footprint than plant-based, as demonstrated in the bar graph above. These emissions are derived from 7 stages of the supply chain: land use change, farming, animal feed, processing, transportation, retail, and packaging.
